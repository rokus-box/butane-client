Introducing Butane:

Because who needs extra security when it comes to your passwords? It's not like they're the keys to your entire online existence or anything. Remembering passwords is overrated. Let Butane do the job while you focus on more important things, like trying to find your misplaced socks.

How it works:

Butane encrypts your passwords on your device using a master password that you provide. This means that your passwords are never sent to the server, and are only decrypted on your device (thus enabling cross-device support).
